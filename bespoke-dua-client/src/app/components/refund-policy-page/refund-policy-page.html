Refund Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Overview

We offer refunds under certain conditions to ensure a fair experience for all users.

2. Web Purchases

If you purchased a subscription through our website, you may request a refund within 7 days of your initial purchase.

Refunds are typically granted if:

  • You experienced a technical issue that prevented use of the service
  • You were charged incorrectly
  • You have not made significant use of the service

We do not generally offer refunds for:

  • Change of mind after extensive usage
  • Partial subscription periods

3. App Store Purchases

If you subscribed through the Apple App Store, all payments and refunds are handled by Apple.

We do not have the ability to issue refunds for App Store purchases. You can request a refund directly through your Apple account.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for a web purchase, contact us at bespokedua@gmail.com.

Please include:

  • Your account email
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for the request

5. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time.
